Daqs Freight Forwarder

Daqs Freight Forwarder is a logistics firm that specializes in shipping balikbayan boxes from Ireland to the Philippines. Since 2001, Daqs Freight Forwarder have helped overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) in Ireland send goods to their loved ones in the Philippines.
