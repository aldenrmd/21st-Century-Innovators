DAQS Freight Forwarder, or Mercury Express as it is known in Ireland, is a shipping and logistics service provider based in Dublin, Ireland. It was founded by Ethel Galorport in 1998 and began as a local courier service. It was initially established to meet the needs of Irish locals. However, in the early 2000s, the Filipino community in Ireland began to grow. Realizing the need for a bridge to the homeland, DAQS Freight Forwarder shifted their focus to meet the demands of the Filipino community in Ireland. More than twenty years later, the company continues to serve the Filipino people.

DAQS Freight Forwarder

DAQS Freight Forwarder is a logistics firm that specializes in shipping balikbayan boxes from Ireland to the Philippines. Since 2001, it has helped overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) in Ireland send goods to their loved ones in the Philippines.
